About Elijah Chacon

Elijah Chacon has dedicated his life to wrestling, earning All-American honors and building a reputation as a high-level competitor. A former state-qualifying wrestler in high school, Elijah continued his athletic career at Indiana Tech, where he competed as a collegiate wrestler.

Transitioning his wrestling success into Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Elijah has used his relentless pressure, takedowns, and mat control to excel in competition. His wrestling foundation has helped him secure championships at CS/RS invitationals and numerous other Jiu-Jitsu tournaments. Known for his competitive mindset and technical approach, Elijah consistently imposes his game on opponents and continues to pursue growth both as an athlete and competitor.
