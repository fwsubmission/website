About

Fort Wayne Submission Fighting is a competition-driven and growth-focused Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy dedicated to developing athletes of all skill levels. Inspired by the proven systems and relentless work ethic of Pedigo Submission Fighting, our gym emphasizes a wrestling-heavy style of jiu-jitsu that thrives in both competition and real-world grappling situations.

We believe success is built through consistency, discipline, and a commitment to constant improvement. Whether your goal is to compete at the highest levels, sharpen your self-defense skills, or simply become a better version of yourself, our coaching and training environment are designed to help you reach your full potential.

At Fort Wayne Submission Fighting, students benefit from a culture that values hard work, technical excellence, and team camaraderie. Our training focuses on aggressive takedowns, dominant top pressure, submission hunting, and the competitive mindset required to excel on the mats.

From beginners taking their first class to experienced competitors preparing for tournaments, Fort Wayne Submission Fighting provides the structure, coaching, and community needed to grow as both an athlete and an individual..
