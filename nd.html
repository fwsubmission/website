About Nick Dietrich

Nick Dietrich is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Brown Belt with over 10 years of training and competition experience. Known for his patient teaching style and calm, composed demeanor, Nick excels at helping students of all skill levels develop confidence and technical proficiency on the mats.

A true Gi specialist, Nick has spent years refining a highly technical and methodical game. His deep understanding of leverage, timing, and positional control allows him to consistently impose his strategy and overcome opponents through precision rather than athleticism alone. Whether coaching beginners or training experienced competitors, Nick brings a wealth of knowledge, professionalism, and a passion for helping others improve their Jiu-Jitsu.
